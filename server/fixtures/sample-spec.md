# Structural Specification - Sample Building (Fixture)

## 1. General

1.1 This specification governs the dimensional and layout requirements for the
primary structural elements of the sample building. All dimensions are in metres
unless stated otherwise.

## 2. Walls

2.1 Exterior walls shall have a minimum thickness of 0.3 m.

2.2 Exterior walls shall have a height of at least 3.0 m.

2.3 Interior partition walls shall not exceed 0.15 m in thickness.

## 3. Slabs and Floors

3.1 Floor slabs shall have a thickness of at least 0.2 m.

3.2 The clear floor-to-ceiling height shall be between 2.7 m and 3.6 m.

## 4. Columns

4.1 Structural columns shall be spaced no more than 6.0 m apart, centre to centre.

4.2 Each column shall have a minimum cross-sectional width of 0.4 m.
